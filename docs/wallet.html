How It Works
1. Your Lattice Address is generated locally on your device.

4. You accumulate LP through contribution, not speculation.

LatticePoints (LP) are an internal contribution metric within the FreeLattice ecosystem. LP are not securities, commodities, currencies, or tokens. LP cannot be purchased, sold, traded, or exchanged for monetary value. LP have no market price. LP represent a measure of meaningful contribution as determined by platform activity.
