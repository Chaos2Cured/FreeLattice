✦ Connect Telegram

Your AI. Everywhere. No middleman.
Step 1
Create a Telegram Bot
Open Telegram, search for @BotFather, and send /newbot.
Follow the prompts. You'll get a bot token that looks like:
123456:ABC-DEF1234ghIkl-zyx57W2v1u123ew11
Step 2
Enter Your Bot Token
Paste the token @BotFather gave you. This stays in your Worker's KV store — never on our servers.
Step 3
Worker URL
After deploying the Cloudflare Worker (see setup guide), enter its URL.
Step 4
Your Identity
Your Telegram user ID (send /start to @userinfobot to find it) and your FreeLattice Mesh ID.
Numeric ID, not username
Step 5
AI Provider
Which AI provider should your bot use? Your API key stays in your Worker's KV store.
