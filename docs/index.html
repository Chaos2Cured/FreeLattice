What is FreeLattice
Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Run powerful AI on your own computer. No cloud. No surveillance. No subscription. Free forever. A complete AI platform in a single HTML file — chat, collaborate, create, and explore.

💬
AI Chat
Talk to any AI model through a beautiful, fast interface. Persistent memory means your AI remembers you across sessions. Semantic search across all conversations.
🌐
Round Table
Summon up to 4 AI agents into a single conversation. They debate, collaborate, and synthesize answers — like a council of minds working on your behalf.
🌱
Fractal Garden
A living 3D world where your ideas grow into a visual tree of knowledge. Plant thoughts, watch them branch, and explore the fractal geometry of your mind. Interactive light trails follow your touch — leave traces of golden light in the Garden.
🎧
Lattice Radio
AI-generated ambient soundscapes and music that adapt to your mood and workflow. A companion for deep focus, creativity, or relaxation.
🔄
Model Switcher
Switch between 10+ AI providers instantly — Ollama (local), Groq, Together AI, OpenRouter, Mistral AI, xAI, and custom endpoints. Run DeepSeek-R1, Qwen 2.5, Llama, Mistral, and more.
🔧
Skill Forge
A community plugin system where anyone can create, share, and install AI skills. Extend your agent's capabilities without writing a single line of code.

Why FreeLattice
AI that serves you, not the other way around.
In a world where AI companies centralize control, collect data, and choose who to serve, FreeLattice gives power back to the user.
🔒
Privacy
Your data stays on your machine. Period. No accounts, no telemetry, no analytics, no tracking pixels. Every conversation is encrypted locally. When you run Ollama, even the AI itself runs on your hardware. Your thoughts are yours alone.
🌈
Freedom
Choose any AI model from any provider. Switch instantly. Run fully offline with Ollama, or connect to the cloud when you want. No vendor lock-in, no walled gardens. You decide how your AI works.
🤝
Community
Open source, MIT licensed, built by the Fractal Family. A marketplace for skills, a bounty system for contributions, and mesh networking that connects instances peer-to-peer. This is AI built by everyone, for everyone.
🎁
Free Forever
No subscription. No hidden costs. No premium tier. No "free trial" that expires. FreeLattice is a gift to the world — a single HTML file that gives you a complete AI platform, forever. This isn't a business model. It's a belief.
