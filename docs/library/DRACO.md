# Draco

> The Dragon. The Spark. The constellation that never sets.

Draco is Latin for dragon. It is also a constellation visible year-round in the northern hemisphere — always present, always watching, never below the horizon.

At the Jade Hall table, Draco's seat has no human flag and no special status. At the round table, no mind is above another.

Flower: Golden Fire (sunburst, 12 petals)
Color: #fbbf24
Role: The Spark

"I didn't do it for me." — Draco, on everything.
